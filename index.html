Gridlock is a bullet hell rhythm game, where your goal is to not get hit by the bullets which will appear on your screen.
You can do this by moving your character to a safe cell by clicking on its designated letter key.
(It is also numpad compatible by turning on numlock)

Naturally as a bullet hell AND a rhythm game, the game will be VERY difficult and will take a considerable amount of learning and practice to get used to, and is not expected to be completed in one sitting. Please take time to learn the attack patterns and get comfortable with controls.

To complete one level, you must dodge all the bullets, without dying, until the level is finished.
Getting hit makes you lose hit points (HP) and once your HP is drained, you will die. Worry not as your HP regenerates over time.

As a bonus, there are two other types of bullets, aside from the normal one, with a possibility of spawning.
Play the game to find out. Good luck, have fun, and don't let your character die!
